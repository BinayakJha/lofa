Who Are We ?

A non-profit organization Literacy of Finance for All (LOFA) works in the fields of economics, business, and financial literacy. With our training programs, orientation sessions, experience sharing programs, and other services, we create a platform for you to discover your hidden potential. We are a group of 40+ dedicated high school students who are now serving on the main committee. We are newly formed organization by high school students for promoting economics, finance and business education among youths. We plan to hold webinars on the Road to Olympiads, Share Market for Beginners, Forex Market and its Working Mechanism, as well as Basic Accounting series and plenty more. Contact Us
